講演概要

Many people say that Beat Saber is one of the best game they played in VR. Not everybody knows that it was made by a small team of three guys in less than a year and half with basically zero development and marketing budget. This talk will cover our journey from beginning until now, including some technical details how the game is working under the hood, process of porting it to PS4 and Oculus Quest, tips for marketing VR games, importance of mixed reality, LBE market introduction and many more.
